Michael Jackson

King of pop

Timeline of Michael Jackson's life:

  • Born in Gary, Indiana in August 29th 1958

  • Joined the Jackson 5 along with brother Marlon in 1964

  • 'I want you back' became first Jackson 5 song to reach no. 1 on the US billboard in 1970

  • Jackson produced his first solo album 'Off the wall' in 1979

  • Jackson released album Thriller in late 1982 which subsequently became the best selling album of all time worlwide, selling an estimated 70 million copies

  • In 1983 , Jackson and his brothers partnered up with PepsiCo in a $5 million promotion deal - a deal that broke records for a celebretiy endorsement at the time.

  • In 1987 Jackson's first album in five years, Bad was highly anticipated, with the industry expecting another major success It became the first album to produce five US number-one singles

  • In Jackson founded the Heal the World Foundation in 1992 . The charity brought underprivileged children to Jackson's ranch to use the theme park rides, and sent millions of dollars around the globe to help children threatened by war, poverty, and disease. That July, Jackson published his second book, Dancing the Dream, a collection of poetry.

  • In late 1993 , Jackson proposed to Lisa Marie Presley, daughter of Elvis Presley. They married in La Vega, Dominican Republic. they separated in December 1995 .

  • Jackson had three kids: Paris Jackson, Prince Michael Jackson II and Prince Michael Jackson

  • On June 25, 2009 , less than three weeks before the first This Is It show was due to begin in London, with all concerts sold out, Jackson died from cardiac arrest aged 50
